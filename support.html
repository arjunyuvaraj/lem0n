Privacy Policy for Basket

Effective Date: September 1st, 2025

Basket is a private app developed and maintained by a single developer, Arjun Yuvaraj. This privacy policy outlines how your data is handled when you use the app.

1. Data Collection

Basket does not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) without your knowledge or consent.

Depending on how you use the app, it may store the following types of data:

  • Items you manually enter (e.g., grocery lists, shopping details)
  • Preferences such as theme settings (e.g., dark mode)

This data is only stored locally on your device or, if connected to Firebase or other services, securely associated with your authenticated account.

2. Third-Party Services

Basket may use trusted third-party services such as:

  • Firebase Authentication: to sign in and manage your user account
  • Firebase Firestore: to store your lists or app data securely in the cloud

These services have their own privacy policies which you can review: Firebase Privacy Policy

No data is shared with third parties for advertising, marketing, or analytics purposes.

3. Data Security

We take your privacy seriously. All data is either:

  • Stored securely on your device, or
  • Stored in your own Firebase-authenticated user space.

Basket does not have access to your private data beyond what is required for basic app functionality.
